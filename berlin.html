About the city

Berlin is the capital of Germany and is located in the northeast of the country, about 70 km from the Polish border.

Berlin is distributed in 12 districts or neighborhoods. They are: Mitte, Friedrichshain - Kreuzberg, Pankow, Charlottenburg - Wilmersdorf, Spandau, Zehlendorf - Steglitz, Schöneberg - Tempelhof, Neukölln, Treptow - Köpenick, Marzahn - Hellersdorf, Lichtenberg and Reinickendorf.

The city has 3,460,725 inhabitants.
